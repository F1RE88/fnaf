Five nights at Freddy's
What is Fnaf

The Five Nights at Freddy's series consists of horror-themed video games
in which the player is usually a night-time employee at a location
connected with Freddy Fazbear's Pizza, a Rotional children's restaurant that takes
inspiration From Family pizza chains like Chuck E. Cheese and Showbiz Pizza Place.
The restaurant has life-size animatronic characters resembling anthropomorphic animals that
perform on a show stage, usually For children's parties. The animatronics
wander the restaurant at night, and the guard is instructed to watch over them.
